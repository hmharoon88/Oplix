Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log in as an owner?

Owners can log in using the "Owner" option on the login screen. Use your registered email and password to access your account.

How do employees clock in/out?

Employees can clock in and out using the Clock In/Out tab in the employee section. Make sure you're logged in and have an active shift.

How do I add a new location?

As an owner, go to the Locations tab and tap the "+" button in the top right corner. Fill in the location details and save.

How do I fill out a lottery form?

Employees must be clocked in first. Then navigate to the Lottery section, select "Active Shift", and fill out the form with the required information.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

How do I manage employees?

As an owner, go to the Employees tab, select a location, and you can add, edit, or manage employees for that location.
